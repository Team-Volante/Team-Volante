Team Volante
Established in 2014, Team Volante is a dynamic student club focused on building innovative Go-Karts and electric buggies for national and international competitions. Originally founded with the goal of designing and constructing Go-Karts, the team has since evolved to compete in eBAJA events, showcasing cutting-edge electric vehicle technology.

Over the years, Team Volante has made significant strides in both the engineering and motorsports arenas. The team has consistently participated in prestigious competitions and earned accolades for their performances. Beyond racing success, the team’s legacy includes several research paper publications by alumni, contributing to the advancement of automotive and electric vehicle technology.

The name "Volante" holds deep significance. In modern Spanish, it refers to the steering wheel, the critical component that guides any four-wheeler. Historically, however, a "Volante" was a one- or two-seater carriage driven by horses in the late 1800s, symbolizing mobility, control, and innovation. This blend of tradition and modern engineering perfectly reflects Team Volante's mission—to combine heritage with forward-thinking technology in motorsport and vehicle design.
