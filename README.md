# Team Volante

Team Volante, established in 2014, is a dynamic student club focused on building innovative Go-Karts and electric buggies for national and international competitions. Originally founded with the goal of designing and constructing Go-Karts, the team has since evolved to compete in **eBAJA** events, showcasing cutting-edge electric vehicle technology.

## Achievements

Over the years, Team Volante has:
- Consistently participated in prestigious competitions.
- Earned accolades and recognition for performance in motorsports.
- Contributed to the academic community with **research paper publications** by alumni.

## The Name: Volante

The term "Volante" carries a deep historical and symbolic significance:
- In **modern-day Spanish**, "Volante" refers to the **steering wheel**, the critical component that guides any four-wheeler.
- Historically, a **Volante** was a **one- or two-seater Spanish carriage** driven by horses in the late 1800s, representing mobility, control, and innovation.

This blend of tradition and modern engineering reflects Team Volante's mission—to combine heritage with forward-thinking technology in motorsport and vehicle design.
