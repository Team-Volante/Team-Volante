Welcome to **Team Volante**, a passionate student club dedicated to pushing the boundaries of engineering and innovation. Established in **2014**, we started as a Go-Kart building team and have evolved into competitors in the **eBAJA** series, designing electric buggies that are both sustainable and high-performance. 🏎️💨

## 🚀 Our Journey

- **Founded**: 2014
- **Competitions**: Go-Karts, eBAJA,Edgeline champions(X2),SAJA E-BAJA,C0C,BFKCT,NEKC etc.
- **Achievements**: Most Awarded racing club in Ramaiah Institute of Technology.
- **Research**: Alumni have contributed to several **published research papers** on automotive technology and innovations.

## 🏆 Achievements

- Competed and won at national and international levels.
- Pioneered the transition from Go-Kart builds to **electric vehicle** design for the **eBAJA** competition.
- Published numerous research papers, contributing to the **automotive and EV** industry.

## 🔍 What's in a Name?

The name "Volante" carries both modern and historical significance:
- In **modern Spanish**, "Volante" translates to **steering wheel**, representing control and direction in a four-wheeler.
- Historically, a **Volante** was a **Spanish carriage** from the late 1800s, symbolizing mobility and innovation.

This mix of **heritage and modern technology** perfectly embodies our mission: to innovate, compete, and advance the future of automotive engineering.
